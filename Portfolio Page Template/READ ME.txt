FAQ:


Q: How do i change the colours?
A: Go to the css folder and open style.css . In there you can find 'navbar','about-info' and all the other classes and change the 'color' tag.

Q: How do i use my logo?
A: Go to the 'img' folder and replace the exisiting logo with yours. //Depending on the size you might need to edit the css a bit. Search for 'navbar-brand'

Q: How do i change the 'Service x'?
A: Simple! Go to the js folder and open the main.js file. Change the Strings, Save, And reload!

Q: How do i change the contact links?
A: Open index.html, And scroll to the bottom, Find where is says '<div class="socialIcons">' and in there you can see all the social icons and links. Change it there!

Q: How do i create another portfolio showcase page?
A: Go to the 'p' folder and copy the exisiting folder 'omegamc' (At time of release) and paste it. Then simply change what you want and remove/add any other features.
